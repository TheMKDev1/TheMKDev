Cable Climbing

A short and simple pixel platformer, where you swing and grapple your way to the finish line.


How to play:

Move Right: D

Move Left: A

Jump: Space

Swing: W

Maniac Tanks

A local multiplayer tank game where 4 players battle to be the last tank standing.


How to play:

Player 1, WASD to move, Q or E to shoot

Player 2, IJKL to move, U or O to shoot

Player 3, TFGH to move, R or Y to shoot

Player 4, Arrow Keys to move, Right Shift to shoot

Variable Warfare

A local multiplayer pvp shooter with a mathematical theme.


How to play:

-P1 (x): WASD

-P2 (y): IJKL

-P3 (z): Arrow Keys

-P4 (w): TFGH
